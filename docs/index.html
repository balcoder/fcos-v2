Financial Control Outsourcing Services

We provide top class financial control services to the retail trade in Ireland

Find Out More

We've got what you need!

Fcos is an innovative Irish company providing financial control outsourcing services to over 50 SuperValu and Centra retailers nationwide. Founded in 2004, Fcos has earned the reputation for excellence in the services we provide. Fcos has invested in the areas of staff training & development and has assembled an ambitious team of professional staff and we are now ideally placed to expand our client base.
